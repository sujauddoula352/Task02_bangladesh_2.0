Pillars Of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Smart Citizens

  • Build an educated, skilled, and tech-aware population
  • Enhance digital literacy, innovation, and employment opportunities

Smart Economy

  • Promote technology-driven industries
  • Boost productivity using AI, robotics, blockchain, and IoT
  • Focus on green economy and renewable energy

Smart Government

  • Deliver all services digitally to citizens.
  • Ensure e-governance, good governance, and transparency.

Smart Society

  • Foster equality, inclusivity, social justice, and sustainable development
  • Modernize health, education, and environmental sectors using technology.

Internationally recognized as the pioneer of microcredit and social business, he has dedicated his life to fighting poverty and empowering marginalized communities, particularly women. His innovative model of financial inclusion transformed millions of lives and earned him the title “Banker to the Poor.”
